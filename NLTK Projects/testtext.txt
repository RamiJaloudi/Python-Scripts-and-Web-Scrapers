Does a respect for autonomy solve the problem of eugenics?

	Introducing autonomy as the guiding principle to the topic of eugenics would certainly curtail the superfluous criticism.  Autonomy will enhance the discussion insofar as the underlying moral issues will be more transparent and genuine.  Nonetheless, criticism will continue to haunt eugenics, like any other issue.
